About Me

I am a task-oriented front-end web developer with a passion for advancing simple solutions to complex processes while maintaining a human-first approach. 

My biggest passion has been using technology to help myself and others expand upon creative possibilities. Some of my hobbies include building apps, servers, and computers, and learning new technology to simplify every day life.

I have an Associate of Arts, and I am currently a student in the University of Arizona Full-Stack Web Developer Boot Camp. Consistently, I am a go-to person for help and advice from my peers, and my professors have remarked multiple times on the high quality of my work. 

I am proficient in popular front-end frameworks. I excel with JavaScript, learning new libraries and frameworks, and interacting with APIs. Additionally, I am also skilled in Node.js development and am currently cross-training for back-end development, with the goal of one day becoming a full-stack developer.
